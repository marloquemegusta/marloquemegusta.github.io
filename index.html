Biography

AI Researcher and Head Researcher
Arquimea Research Center (ARC) & Universidad Rey Juan Carlos (URJC), Spain

I am an AI researcher and hands-on technical lead working at the intersection of volumetric capture, neural rendering, NeRFs, 3D Gaussian Splatting, relighting, 3D scene editing/generation, and photorealistic simulation. I currently serve as Head Researcher at Arquimea Research Center while pursuing doctoral research affiliated with Universidad Rey Juan Carlos.

My work combines peer-reviewed research with practical ownership of research systems: building and validating prototypes, guiding research lines, improving experiment workflows, and helping ML researchers use heterogeneous compute, GPUs, internal tooling, and AI-assisted development workflows more effectively. I approach generative AI and coding assistants as technical tools to evaluate, test, debug, and integrate carefully, not as substitutes for engineering ownership.

I am also involved in open-source and technical communication as co-founder and organizer of ML Circle / Madrid Machine Learning Meetup, and through small technical projects such as PocketDither.
